02
Phase Two
ONBOARDING & ACCESS
Day 0–7 · First week
Get the direct line open, get analytics access, and book the onboarding strategy call — before a single word of strategy is written.
ADD CLIENT TO WHATSAPP / SLACK
Team Within 24 hrs
Get the direct line open immediately. This is how they'll reach you — make it feel personal from the start.
  • Add client on WhatsApp or invite to your shared Slack channel (confirm which they prefer)
  • Send a quick intro message — keep it warm and brief
WhatsApp / Slack intro message
Hey [Name]! It's the Ampvox team 👋 This is your direct line throughout your retainer — use it for anything. Your first strategy pack lands within the next 3 days. Excited to get started.
REQUEST ANALYTICS ACCESS
Team Within 24 hrs
You can't write week-one reviews without data. Get access requested immediately — follow up if not received within 48 hours.
  • Email or message requesting view access to all active platform analytics (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube etc.)
  • Send a simple screen-share guide if they're unsure how to grant access
  • If running paid ads — request read-only ad account access
  • Set a reminder: if not received in 48 hrs, chase once via WhatsApp/Slack
Analytics access request — message
Hi [Name], to get your weekly reviews running from day one we need view access to your social analytics. Could you grant access for: [list their platforms]. Happy to jump on a 5-min call if easier! — Ampvox team
BOOK ONBOARDING STRATEGY CALL
Team Within 48 hrs Calendly
This call happens at the start of the retainer — before the first strategy is built. It's how you understand their current situation, goals, and priorities so the strategy is built around reality from day one.
  • Send your Calendly link for a 45-min call, targeting day 3–7 of their first cycle
  • Frame it as an onboarding call — not a strategy review (that comes end of month)
  • If they don't book within 3 days, nudge via WhatsApp/Slack
  • Once booked — add to Google Calendar with a prep reminder 24hrs before
  • On the call: cover their current situation, goals, upcoming plans, and any content they're proud of or embarrassed by
  • Use findings to inform the Month 1 strategy before building it
Calendly booking message
Hi [Name] — before we build your first strategy, we want to get properly under the hood. Let's book a quick 45-min onboarding call so we can understand exactly where you're at and build everything around that. Grab a time here: [Calendly link]
03
Phase Three
MONTH ONE DELIVERY
Day 7–10 · Post onboarding call
Now you know their situation. Build the strategy around what you learned on the call — then deliver it.
AUDIT EXISTING CONTENT
Team Day 1–2
Before building anything, understand what they've already got. Pull the data and look for patterns.
  • Review last 30–60 days of content across all platforms
  • Pull top 5 performing posts — note format, hook, topic, length
  • Pull 5 lowest performing posts — note the same
  • Check posting frequency, consistency, and brand voice alignment
  • Drop findings into their Notion workspace
BUILD & SEND MONTH 1 STRATEGY PACK
Team By day 3 Drive + Gmail
The centrepiece of month one. Strategy document + content calendar, built using onboarding call findings and delivered clean and on time.
  • Use onboarding call notes to inform strategic direction
  • Build the monthly content strategy doc in Google Drive
  • Build the 30-day content calendar (post types, hooks, timing, platform)
  • Review and approve both before sending
  • Send via email with a short personal note — not just an attachment dump
Strategy delivery email — subject
Your Month 1 strategy is here — [Brand Name] 🎯 Hi [Name], Your first monthly strategy and content calendar are ready. Everything is built around what we've seen in your analytics and where you want to go. Attached: → Month 1 Content Strategy → 30-Day Content Calendar A few things worth flagging: [2–3 key strategic points — keep it human, not corporate] Any questions — you know where we are. The Ampvox team
04
Phase Four
WEEK 1 REVIEW
Day 7 · Every Monday
First weekly performance review. Sets the cadence. Every Monday from here on.
PULL WEEKLY PERFORMANCE DATA
Team Every Monday AM
Pull the numbers every Monday morning before the review is sent. Keep it consistent — same metrics, same format, every week.
  • Log into all client platform analytics — screenshot or export key numbers
  • Track: reach, engagement rate, follower change, top post of the week
  • Compare to previous week — flag anything that's moved significantly
  • Fill in weekly row in their Notion performance tracker
SEND WEEKLY REVIEW MESSAGE
Team Every Monday, by 10am WhatsApp / Slack
Send via WhatsApp or Slack — not email. Keep it concise and actionable. 3–5 lines max, then a clear "this week focus."
  • Lead with the biggest win or most interesting data point
  • Flag one thing that underperformed and why
  • Give one clear action or focus for the coming week
Weekly review message template
📊 Weekly Review — w/c [Date] ✅ Win: [Top performing post / metric] 📉 Watch: [One underperformer + quick reason why] 🎯 This week: [One clear focus / action] Keep going — [Name]. Any questions, drop them here.
05
Phase Five
END OF MONTH ONE
Day 25–30
Strategy call, monthly report, and seamless rollover into month two. Leave them excited for what's next.
BUILD MONTHLY REPORT
Team Day 24–25 Notion / Drive
Complete the monthly report before the strategy call — so you walk into the call with the data already structured.
  • Fill in the monthly report template (from the onboarding pack)
  • Pull full month metrics: followers, reach, engagement, posts published, link clicks
  • Identify top 3 performing pieces of content + top 3 learnings
  • Draft initial strategic recommendations for Month 2
RUN MONTH-END STRATEGY CALL
Team Day 25–28 Google Meet / Zoom
45 minutes. Walk through the report together, celebrate wins, address anything that wasn't working, and set direction for Month 2.
  • Screen share the monthly report — walk through section by section
  • Open floor: let them talk — what felt right, what felt off, what they want more of
  • Agree on 2–3 strategic priorities for Month 2
  • Note any upcoming launches, events or campaigns to build around
  • Record the call — send recording + written summary within 24 hours
  • Book next month's call before hanging up
SEND REPORT + CALL SUMMARY
Team Within 24 hrs of call Gmail
Within 24 hours of the strategy call, the client gets a complete wrap-up in their inbox.
  • Email: month-end report PDF + call recording link + written action summary
  • Written summary: bullet points — what we agreed, what they need to do, what we're doing for Month 2
  • End with a forward-looking line about Month 2 — keep momentum high
