Week by week
YOUR MONTHLY CYCLE
Days 1–3
STRATEGY DELIVERY
New Cycle, New Strategy
  • Ampvox delivers your fresh monthly content strategy within 3 days of your billing anniversary
  • Plug-and-play content calendar with post types, hooks, and timings lands in your inbox
  • Any new platform updates or algorithm shifts flagged and factored in
  • You: Review strategy, flag any upcoming launches or events we should build around
Week 1–4
WEEKLY REVIEWS
Eyes On Your Numbers Every Week
  • Ampvox sends a written weekly performance snapshot every Monday
  • Covers: reach, engagement rate, follower growth, top-performing content
  • Flags what to double down on, what to cut, and what to test
  • Real-time messaging support available throughout — ask us anything
Ongoing
TREND & ALGO UPDATES
Staying Ahead of the Platform
  • When platforms make significant changes, you'll hear from us first
  • Trending formats and sounds flagged as they peak — not after they're dead
  • Follow our What's Trending channel on Instagram (@ampvoxmedia) for weekly trend drops between our updates
  • Collaboration and partnership opportunities surfaced as they arise
  • Brand outreach guidance and scripts provided when relevant
Week 4
STRATEGY CALL
45-Min Monthly Debrief & Planning Session
  • Full month review — wins, learnings, and what the data is telling us
  • Set strategic priorities and focus areas for the coming month
  • Open floor: bring your questions, new ideas, or anything you want to cover
  • Recording sent within 24 hours with a written action plan summary
End of Cycle
MONTHLY REPORT
Your Monthly Performance Report
  • Full month growth summary: follower growth, reach, engagement, top content
