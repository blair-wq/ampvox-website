Common Questions

GOT
QUESTIONS?

Who is Ampvox best suited for? +

Ampvox works with two audiences. The Agency Track is designed for established B2C brands, typically sustainable fashion, clean beauty, wellness, DTC food brands, with 10K–50K followers who post regularly but have hit a growth ceiling. The Education Track is for solo entrepreneurs, content creators, and personal brands who want to build world-class social skills themselves.

Do you create the content for us? +

No, Ampvox is a strategy and education agency. We build your content system, scripts, hooks, calendars, and brand voice, and you execute. This is intentional. Brands that understand their own content grow faster and more sustainably than those who outsource it entirely. We give you the blueprint; you become the expert.

What's the difference between the 30-Day Intensive and the Retainer? +

The 30-Day Intensive is a one-time comprehensive overhaul, strategy, content system, scripts, hooks, and direct support all built in 30 days. The Monthly Retainer is available to Intensive graduates only, and provides ongoing strategic direction, performance reviews, and accountability as you scale. Think of the Intensive as the foundation; the Retainer as the momentum engine.

What sectors do you work with? +

We only work with mission-driven, positive-impact brands. Our sweet spot is sustainable fashion, clean beauty, wellness, DTC food, and education. We don't work with vaping brands, fast fashion, gambling, or industries that conflict with our values. If you're not sure if you qualify, just email us.

Is there a payment plan available? +

Yes. The 30-Day Intensive has a payment plan option of 3 × £550. The Collective has a 3-month upfront option at £697 (saving £194). All other offerings are billed as listed. Get in touch if you'd like to discuss options.
